About IJMER Journal

International Journal of Modern Engineering Research (IJMER) is Peer reviewed, online Journal. It serves as an international archival forum of scholarly research related to engineering and science education.

International Journal of Modern Engineering Research (IJMER) covers all the fields of engineering and science: Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Agricultural Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, Thermodynamics, Structural Engineering, Control Engineering, Robotics, Mechatronics, Fluid Mechanics, Nanotechnology, Simulators, Web-based Learning, Remote Laboratories, Engineering Design Methods, Education Research, Students' Satisfaction and Motivation, Global Projects, and Assessment.... About IJMER

Our Mission

IJMER serves as an international archival forum of scholarly research related to engineering and science education. Our mission is to promote and support research excellence in all fields of engineering and technology.

Publication Ethics

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of publication ethics and follow strict guidelines to ensure the integrity of all published work. Our peer-review process ensures quality and originality in all published papers.

Impact Factor

IJMER has consistently maintained a high impact factor, reflecting the quality and influence of the research papers published in our journal. We are indexed in major academic databases and citation indices.

Open Access Policy

We believe in making research freely accessible to all. Our open access policy ensures that all published articles are immediately and permanently free for everyone to read and download.
